L298N Motor Driver Basics

Features:
- Dual H-Bridge motor driver
- Can control 2 DC motors or 1 stepper motor
- Logic voltage: 5V
- Motor voltage: up to 35V

Common Issues:
- Motor not spinning
- Driver overheating
- Low torque

Troubleshooting:
- Check ENA / ENB pins for PWM signals
- Verify power supply for motors
- Use proper heat sink if current is high
- Ensure correct wiring of IN1, IN2, IN3, IN4
