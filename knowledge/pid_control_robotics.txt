PID Control in Robotics

PID stands for:
- Proportional
- Integral
- Derivative

Purpose:
Maintain stable control of systems like motors, drones, or robotic arms.

Common Tuning Issues:
- Overshoot
- Oscillation
- Slow response

Basic Tuning Strategy:
1. Increase P until system responds quickly.
2. Add D to reduce oscillations.
3. Add I to remove steady-state error.
